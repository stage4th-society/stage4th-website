descriptionZh: "有些剧本写在很久以前，却从未真正离开舞台。跨越不同的时代与文化，这些作品依然回应着今天我们熟悉的问题：关系、身份、自由、社会期待，以及人在压力之下作出的选择。\n\n这个剧本朗读系列将重新走近经典短剧。我们并不把它们视为停留在过去的文本，而是把它们看作与当下持续对话的作品。通过简约的舞台呈现与专注的表演，我们邀请观众重新聆听那些仍在日常生活之下回响的语言、沉默、张力与幽默。\n\n首场朗读《小事之重》将呈现苏珊·格拉斯佩尔的两部独幕剧：《压抑的欲望》与《琐事》。这两部早期现代短剧彼此呼应：一部以犀利的讽刺揭示过度分析的荒诞，另一部则在安静的张力中展开那些被忽略、未被听见、也未曾说出口的事。"
descriptionEn: "Some plays were written long ago, but they have never truly left the stage. Across different times and cultures, these works continue to speak to questions that feel familiar today: relationships, identity, freedom, social expectations, and the choices people make under pressure.\n\nThis staged reading series revisits classic short plays not as relics of the past, but as living conversations with the present. Through minimal staging and focused performance, we invite audiences to listen closely to the language, silence, tension, and humour that still resonate beneath everyday life.\n\nThe first reading, The Weight of Small Things, brings together Susan Glaspell’s Suppressed Desires and Trifles — two early modern one-act plays in conversation. One uses sharp satire to expose the absurdity of over-analysis; the other builds quiet tension around what is overlooked, unheard, and left unsaid."
playwrightZh: "Susan Glaspell 是美国剧作家、小说家与记者，也是20世纪初女性主义戏剧的重要开拓者。她最著名的作品包括《Trifles》（《琐事》），通过一个谋杀案件揭示女性经验与视角在父权结构中的被忽视，以及与 George Cram Cook 合作的讽刺喜剧《Suppressed Desires》（《被压抑的欲望》）。"
playwrightEn: "Susan Glaspell was an American playwright, novelist, and journalist, and a pioneering figure in early 20th-century feminist theatre. She is best known for Trifles, a landmark one-act play that reveals how women's experiences and perspectives are often overlooked within patriarchal systems, and for Suppressed Desires, a satirical comedy co-written with George Cram Cook."
